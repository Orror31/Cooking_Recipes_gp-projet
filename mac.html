Mac and Cheese Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound elbow macaroni
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. Cook the macaroni according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  4. Add the flour and whisk until smooth.
  5. Add the milk, cream, salt, black pepper, and cayenne pepper. Whisk until smooth.
  6. Cook the sauce, whisking constantly, until it thickens, about 5 minutes.
  7. Remove the sauce from the heat and stir in the cheddar cheese and Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  8. Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce and stir to combine.
  9. Pour the mac and cheese into the prepared baking dish.
  10. Sprinkle the panko breadcrumbs over the top of the mac and cheese.
  11. Bake for 25-30 minutes, until the top is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  12. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before serving.

Notes

  • You can use any type of pasta you like for this recipe, but elbow macaroni is traditional.
  • You can use any type of cheese you like for this recipe, but cheddar and Parmesan are classic choices.
  • You can add other ingredients to this recipe, such as bacon, ham, or vegetables, to make it your own.

Enjoy your Mac and Cheese!
